# Privacy Policy for YouTube Playlist Backup

**Last updated:** January 24, 2026

## Overview

YouTube Playlist Backup is a browser extension that helps you monitor your YouTube playlists and track when videos become unavailable. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

This extension collects and stores the following data **locally in your browser only**:

- YouTube playlist metadata (titles, descriptions, thumbnails)
- Video metadata from your playlists (titles, channels, thumbnails, video IDs)
- Your sync preferences (daily/weekly)

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API. **No data is sent to external servers** other than:

- **YouTube Data API** (googleapis.com) - to fetch your playlist information
- **Google OAuth** - to authenticate your Google account

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties. The extension does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising.

## Permissions Used

- **identity**: Required for Google sign-in to access your YouTube playlists
- **storage**: Store playlist data locally in your browser
- **alarms**: Schedule automatic background sync
- **host_permissions (googleapis.com)**: Communicate with YouTube Data API

## Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:
1. Removing the extension from Chrome
2. Clearing browser data for the extension
